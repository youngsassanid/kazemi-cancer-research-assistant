About the System

Comprehensive Cancer Research Platform

The Kazemi Cancer Research Assistant is a sophisticated system designed for advanced cancer research and analysis. It combines multiple machine learning models with clinical data management to provide insights into patient prognosis, treatment recommendations, and research opportunities.

Important Notice

This software is for research and educational purposes only. It is not intended for clinical use. Always consult qualified oncologists for medical decisions.

Technical Architecture

Built with modern web technologies, the system features:

  • Multiple ML models for prognosis prediction
  • Electronic Health Record simulation
  • Clinical trial matching algorithms
  • Drug interaction databases
  • Data visualization capabilities
