About the Platform

Comprehensive Oncology Research Suite

Built for researchers, oncologists, and students, this platform integrates cutting-edge AI with clinical data systems to accelerate cancer research and improve decision support.

Important Disclaimer

For research and educational use only. This tool is not FDA-approved for clinical decision-making. Always consult qualified medical professionals.

Technical Stack

  • Ensemble ML: Random Forest, XGBoost, Neural Networks
  • EHR: SQLite with encrypted patient records
  • Trials: Real-time API integration with ClinicalTrials.gov
  • Drugs: DrugBank + interaction ontology
  • Viz: Plotly.js interactive dashboards
